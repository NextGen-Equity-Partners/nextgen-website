How we work

How we work with entrepreneurs.

We listen before we form opinions. Keep the process as simple as possible. And stay with you after closing — not only in the early phase.

Getting to know

Time for the company
– not for models.

Before we talk about structures, valuations, or numbers, it's about the company itself — the history, the people, the markets where it is strong and where it could change.

We take time for this phase. It is the foundation for everything that follows — and the only way to genuinely contribute more than capital.

01

First conversation

Personal, confidential, without pressure. We understand the starting point and goals — and say openly if we are not the right partner.

02

Tieferes Getting to know

Mehrere Gespräche mit Unternehmern, Geschäftsführung und wichtigen Personen im Unternehmen. Besuch vor Ort, wenn es passt. Kein Fragenkatalog – Verständnis.

03

Initial view

We share early how we see the company, what valuation we consider realistic, and what a joint collaboration could look like.

Cooperating

When we say yes,
it stands.

Once both sides want to move on, we keep the process as lean and predictable as possible. Clear valuation, clear structures, clear timeline. No valuation games, no last-minute renegotiation.

There is a different form of collaboration for every situation — what matters is that it fits the company and the people.

01

Nachfolgelösungen

Diskret, verlässlich, langfristig. Wir tragen die Responsibility weiter, ohne den Charakter des Unternehmens zu verändern.

02

Corporate carve-outs

Untangle structures, preserve substance and team. A new framework in which the business can keep growing independently.

03

Growth financing

Capital and operational support for the next step — without the owners having to give up the wheel.

04

Management partnerships

Together with managers who want to take over a company or grow into a central role.

Building

The first months together.

After closing, it's not about big changes — it's about a shared understanding: where the company stands, where it wants to go, and where we can concretely help. Three focus areas we align with owners and managers, not decide over their heads.

01 · Clarity

A shared roadmap.

We sit down with the leadership team and prioritize: what has the biggest effect over the next twelve months — from the perspective of customers, team, and economics?

02 · Operational support

Hands, not just slides.

Where it helps, we get involved — whether in reporting and control, in acquisitions, or in introducing digital tools in daily operations.

03 · Digitalization

Where it makes sense.

Our in-house digitalization team works directly inside the company — on real processes, with measurable results. Not a strategy project that ends up in a folder.
